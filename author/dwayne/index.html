News: Sony Playstation/Super Famicom

06.06.2007 2132America/Los_Angeles | dwayne

A website called Game-Rave.com has put up what it says are prototype images of the Sony PlayStation/Super Famicom. That was the CD-based console Nintendo and Sony planned to collaborate on. According to the console database, this system was intended to play both Super Famicom games and Playstation games, albeit a different breed of PlayStation than what we know today. This was found via the lost levels forums, and sure is covered with watermarks.

News: Melding of genres infuses new hope for gamers

22.05.2007 2035America/Los_Angeles | dwayne

R-Type Final may have been the last shooter in Irem’s series, but it wasn’t the last R-Type game. The newly announced “R-Type Tactics” for PSP falls into the genre of “hex-oriented turn-based strategy” – not exactly what I was expecting, but fresh none-the-less. Perhaps following in the footsteps of Cosmic Wars and Paro Wars?
